### Bluetooth Connection

Open the Bluetooth page in the settings app on your phone. Make sure Bluetooth is on, then connect to the available device named `AL Hydrabuds ANC`.

![Illustration](插图/Manual12_7.jpg)

The phone diagram shows Bluetooth set to ON and the available device list containing `AL Hydrabuds ANC`.

Note: If the earbuds have previously been paired to a nearby device, they automatically reconnect to it.

### Multipoint Bluetooth Connection

With multipoint connection, you can connect up to two devices to the earbuds, such as a smartphone and a computer.

1. On the device already paired to the earbuds, open the Bluetooth menu and turn Bluetooth off. The earbuds disconnect from that device and become available for pairing to a second device.
2. Pair the earbuds to the second device.
3. Turn Bluetooth back on on the first device. The earbuds automatically reconnect to it. You now have two devices paired and can use either one for music or calls.

## Earbud Controls

The Hydrabuds ANC click each time you perform a single press.

### Controlling Music

![Illustration](插图/Manual12_8.jpg)

Music controls:

- Play or pause: press either earbud once.
- Volume up: press the left earbud twice.
- Volume down: press the right earbud twice.
- Previous track: press the left earbud three times.
- Next track: press the right earbud three times.

### Controlling Calls

![Illustration](插图/Manual12_9.jpg)

Call controls:

- Answer a call: press either earbud once.
- End a call: press and hold either earbud.
- Reject a call: press and hold either earbud when the call comes in.

## More Controls

### Phone Voice Assistant

To activate the phone voice assistant, press and hold the left earbud until the first beep, then let go.

![Illustration](插图/earphones_01.png)

The diagram shows a press-and-hold gesture on the left earbud for the phone voice assistant.

### Spotify Tap

To activate Spotify Tap, press and hold the right earbud until the first beep, then let go. The Spotify app resumes playback where you left off. If there was no previous session, or to play other music, tap again and Spotify will play songs it thinks you will like.

![Illustration](插图/earphones_02.png)

The diagram shows a press-and-hold gesture on the right earbud for Spotify Tap.

### Ambient Awareness and ANC

To turn ambient awareness and ANC modes on or off, press the left earbud. The earbud cycles through the modes.

![Illustration](插图/earphones_03.png)

The diagram shows a single press on the left earbud to cycle ambient awareness and active noise canceling modes.

### Low Latency Mode

Low Latency Mode reduces the latency, or time delay, inherent in Bluetooth connections. It is best for lip sync and gaming. To turn Low Latency Mode on or off, press and hold until the second beep, then let go.

![Illustration](插图/Manual12_10.jpg)

The diagram shows the low-latency gesture on either earbud: press and hold the left or right earbud until the second beep, then release.

### Pair a New Device

Turn Bluetooth off on your phone or device. The earphones go into pairing mode. Alternatively, press and then press and hold either the left or right earbud to drop the current connection and enter pairing mode.

### Reset

The earbuds must be disconnected from Bluetooth and removed from the case before resetting.

To perform a factory reset, press and hold for 6 seconds. This erases all settings.

![Illustration](插图/Manual12_11.jpg)

The factory-reset diagram shows both earbuds outside the case, labeled L and R, with flashing red and blue lights.

To perform a hardware reset, which restarts the earphones, press and hold for 10 seconds. Press again to power on.

![Illustration](插图/Manual12_12.jpg)

The hardware-reset diagram also shows both earbuds flashing red and blue. Hardware resets are only necessary in the rare case that the earbuds lock up and are unresponsive.

## Hydrabuds App and Voice Control

### Hydrabuds App

Scan the QR code in the original quick start guide to download the Hydrabuds App for iOS or Android. The QR area in the PDF is marked `FPO`, indicating a placeholder in the source document.

Via the app, you can enable hands-free voice control, customize button actions, browse and enable advanced features, and update the firmware when necessary. When the app notifies you, update to the latest firmware to ensure the best performance and user experience.

### Voice Control

Once voice control is enabled in the Hydrabuds App, say "Hey Hydra" and you will hear a confirmation tone. Use the commands below to control the Hydrabuds ANC by voice command. A second tone is heard after a successful command.

Say "Hey Hydra" followed by one of these commands:

- "Play"
- "Pause"
- "Next"
- "Volume Up"
- "Volume Down"
- "Ambient ON"
- "Ambient OFF"
- "Noise Canceling ON"
- "Noise Canceling OFF"
- "Spotify"
- "Assistant"
- "End Call"

If a call is coming in, you can also say these commands without first saying "Hey Hydra":

- "Accept"
- "Reject"

## Maintenance and Care

- Use a soft cloth or paper towel to clean the earphones. Never use harsh chemicals or detergents for cleaning. Make sure the earphones are dry before charging.
- When the earphones are not in use, store them in a cool, dry place.
- Never tug or yank on a cable while it is connected to the charging case. Connect and disconnect cables as carefully as possible.
- Never expose the earphones to high temperatures or extreme cold.
- Recycle or dispose of the earphones properly according to local laws and rules. Contact local recycling facilities or the manufacturer for further information.
