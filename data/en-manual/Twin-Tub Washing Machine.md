# Twin-Tub Washing Machine Owner's Instructions

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_0.jpg)

Before operating the unit, please read this manual thoroughly and retain it for future reference.

## Features

### Powerful washing by large pulsator

This washing machine has a large pulsator so that it can wash evenly. The three-way lint filter strains off lint efficiently during washing, so you can wash laundry cleanly. The normal or soft selector lets you wash laundry efficiently with either Normal or Soft action.

## Cautions

Be sure that the washer is grounded. To avoid electrical shock, use a metal pipe for the ground connection. Do not ground the washer with gas pipes or telephone lines, to avoid the danger of explosion or lightning strike.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_1.jpg)

Do not put your hands into the spin dryer basket during spinning.

![Illustration](插图/washing_machine1_02.jpg)

Your fingers can be caught by spinning laundry and injured. Never allow children to operate or play with the washer. Children playing with the washer may accidentally fall into the washing tub.

![Illustration](插图/washing_machine1_01.jpg)

Disconnect the power cord from the power supply after use. Ventilation openings must not be obstructed by carpeting when the washing machine is installed on a carpeted floor. If the supply cord of this appliance is damaged, it must be replaced only by an authorized technician appointed by the manufacturer, because special purpose tools are required. To avoid overfilling and damage, this appliance must be filled with the operator present, using a moderate water supply pressure only.

## Precautions when operating

#### Don't use excessively hot water

Water that is 50°C or hotter may deform or damage plastic parts. Clothing may also be deformed or discolored.

#### Before washing, empty all pockets

If nails or pins remain in pockets, they may damage the washer or clothes. Close the water tap a little if the water pressure is too high. Be sure to cover laundry with the inner cover before spinning. This prevents laundry from being tossed out and damaged. To avoid water splashes, close the wash tub lid. Never splash water on the control panel.

## Description of Parts

Overflow filter, lint filter, wash tub, wash tub lid, spin dryer lid, spin dryer basket, spin dryer hatch, power cord, drain filter, earth wire, water drain hose, and pulsator. The plug shape may not correspond with the drawing.

Keep the lid closed during washing and spinning. When you open the spin dryer lid while the spin basket is spinning, the spinning is stopped by the brake system.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_2.jpg)

### Control panel

1. WASH SELECTOR: Select wash action.

2. WASH TIMER: Set the wash time, 1 to 15 minutes.

3. CYCLE SELECTOR: Set washing, rinsing, or draining.

4. SPIN DRY TIMER: Set the spin time, 1 to 5 minutes (or 1 to 10 minutes on some models).

5. WATER SELECTOR: During washing, select Wash. During spin rinsing, select Spin.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_3.jpg)

## Washing procedure

### To wash

1. Before washing, connect the water supply hose and open the water tap. Lay the drain hose down toward a sinkhole for models without a drain pump, or place the drain hose in a sink or bath for models with a drain pump. Connect the power cord to the power supply outlet.

2. Set the WASH SELECTOR KNOB to the desired mode.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_4.jpg)

3. Set the WATER SELECTOR knob to WASH.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_5.jpg)

4. Set the CYCLE SELECTOR knob to WASH-RINSE. For models with a drain pump, select WASH.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_6.jpg)

5. Fill water in the washtub and add the detergent.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_7.jpg)

6. Load laundry in the wash tub and fill water to the `H` high water level.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_8.jpg)

7. Set the WASH TIMER for 1 to 15 minutes.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_9.jpg)

8. After washing, set the CYCLE SELECTOR knob to DRAIN.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_10.jpg)

### Spin dry

1. Transfer the clothes into the spin basket and arrange the clothes evenly.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_11.jpg)

2. Be sure to attach the safety cover and close the inner lid.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_12.jpg)

3. Close the spin dryer lid.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_13.jpg)

4. Set the SPIN DRY TIMER for 1 to 5 minutes, or 1 to 10 minutes on some models.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_14.jpg)

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_15.jpg)

**Note:** The two timer images show the 1-5 minute and 1-10 minute model variants.

### Rinsing

1. Set the CYCLE SELECTOR knob to WASH-RINSE. For models with a drain pump, select WASH.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_16.jpg)

2. Supply the proper amount of water, without overflowing the top of the washing tub.

3. Set the WASH TIMER for 2 to 3 minutes.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_17.jpg)

4. Drain the water. Set the CYCLE SELECTOR knob to DRAIN.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_18.jpg)

5. Repeat this cycle until the drain water is clean.

### Overflow rinse

1. Keep supplying water. Adjust water pressure to ensure that the amount of water supplied does not exceed the amount of water drained.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_19.jpg)

2. Set the WASH TIMER for 6-8 minutes.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_20.jpg)

3. As soon as the WASH TIMER shuts off, turn off the water tap.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_21.jpg)

4. Set the CYCLE SELECTOR knob to `DRAIN`.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_22.jpg)

### Additional rinse

1. After rinsing, you can select this course for more effective rinsing. Do not select only this course for rinsing. Load the laundry evenly into the spin basket.

2. Set the WATER SELECTOR knob to `SPIN` and the CYCLE SELECTOR knob to `DRAIN`.

3. Supply water for about 1 minute. Adjust the water flow to prevent overflow from the wash tub.

4. After stopping the water supply, cover the laundry with the safety cover and close the inner lid.

5. Close the spin dryer lid.

6. Set the SPIN DRY TIMER for 2-3 minutes.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_23.jpg)

## Installation

### Environment

#### Leave some space

Leave at least 15 cm between the washer and the wall.

#### Place the washer on a sturdy flat surface

If the washer is placed on an uneven or weak surface, noise or vibration may occur.

#### Never install the washer near water

Do not place the washer in steamy rooms or where the washer is directly exposed to rain. Moisture may destroy the electrical insulation and cause an electrical shock hazard.

#### Avoid direct sunlight or heating devices

As plastic and electrical components are affected by direct heat, never place the washer near heaters, boilers, or similar sources. Do not place it under direct sunlight. For pump models, install the drain hose about 70 to 80 cm above the ground.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_24.jpg)

### Connecting the drain hose

1. Before connecting the drain hose, remove the cover from the drain hole.

2. After pressing the joint ring, insert the drain hose in the drain direction.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_25.jpg)

### Mounting the legs

Lay down the washer with its back facing downward. Insert four legs into the leg mounting sections at the base corners of the main body. Insert the claw of each leg into the square hole of the leg mounting section, and push the leg in the arrow direction shown in the figure until it clicks.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_26.jpg)

## Cleaning the machine

### Cleaning the lint filter

If the lint filter is filled after washing, push it down and pull it out as shown in the figure. Remove the collected lint and rinse the filter net.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_27.jpg)

### Drain filter

If draining is incomplete, disassemble the drain filter with a screwdriver and remove lint from the filter as shown.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_28.jpg)

### Overflow filter

Pull the upper side of the overflow filter with two fingers in the direction of the arrow as shown in the figure.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_29.jpg)

> The figure labels the top catch as `A`.

### Cleaning the washtub

First, fill water in the washtub to the proper level, then drain water from the washtub while operating the wash cycle for 1 to 2 minutes. Close the washtub lid.

### Cleaning the washer body

Wipe stains on the cabinet and operation panel with a soft cloth. Do not use benzene, thinner, cleanser, or wax, and do not scrub the washer with a brush. Painted surfaces or plastic parts will be damaged.

## Maintenance

### When you load the laundry into the spin basket

Press the laundry evenly and put the safety cover into the spin basket drum as shown in the figure.

![Illustration](插图/Manual39_33.jpg)

### To pick out clothing that has fallen between the basket and tub

For safety, pull the power supply plug out of the socket.

1. Remove the screws from the spin dryer hatch.

2. The spin dryer hatch is fixed to the brim by the concave and convex parts at two places. Expand the concave and convex parts and pull up the spin dryer hatch to remove it.

3. Remove the clothing.

4. Close the spin dryer hatch. Push the hatch firmly until the catch at the front is engaged securely.
