Question: 使用发电机前，对发动机机油进行使用前检查十分重要，正确的步骤是什么？

## Routing
- `agentic-rag/ch-manual/手册内容总览.md:96` indicates `发电机手册.md` is the relevant manual for generator-related questions.

## Primary evidence
- `agentic-rag/ch-manual/发电机手册.md:134-136` states every use requires a pre-use check.
- `agentic-rag/ch-manual/发电机手册.md:136-137` warns that engine and muffler stay hot after operation.
- `agentic-rag/ch-manual/发电机手册.md:151-158` states to confirm engine oil is at the upper limit of the oil filler opening, add oil if needed, and recommends API SE or SF grade oil.
- `agentic-rag/ch-manual/发电机手册.md:160-163` states the generator ships without engine oil and must be filled before starting.

## Supporting evidence
- `agentic-rag/ch-manual/发电机手册.md:95-97` explains the oil warning system: if oil is low the engine may stop and the oil warning lamp may blink for several seconds; add oil before restarting.

## Interpretation
The correct pre-use engine oil check is:
1) place the generator on a level surface and let it cool,
2) check that engine oil is at the upper limit of the oil filler opening,
3) add oil to the upper limit if it is low,
4) use API SE or SF grade oil.
