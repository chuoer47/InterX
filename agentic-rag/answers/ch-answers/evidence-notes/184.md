# Evidence Notes for Question 184

## Question
水泵安全排放燃油的正确步骤是什么？

## Source Manual
水泵手册.md (Water Pump Manual)

## Relevant Sections

### 1. Safety Information (Lines 22-29)
- Fuel is highly flammable and toxic
- Turn off engine before refueling
- No smoking or open flames near fuel
- Don't spill fuel on engine or muffler
- Keep machine upright during operation/transport

### 2. Fuel Discharge Procedure (Lines 295-300)
Title: 排放燃油

Steps:
1. 排空油箱、燃油开关及化油器浮子室
2. 向油箱内倒入一杯SAE 10W30或20W40发动机机油
3. 晃动油箱使内壁均匀附着机油
4. 排出多余机油
