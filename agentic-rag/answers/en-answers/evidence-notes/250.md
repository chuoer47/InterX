# Evidence Notes: 250

Question: How do I use the jet wash function to clean the boat after using it?

Manual guess: Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md

Evidence refs:
- agentic-rag/en-manual/Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md:1385-1393
- agentic-rag/en-manual/Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md:1399-1425
- agentic-rag/en-manual/Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md:1429-1437

Answer basis:
- The Jet wash section warns that the system must be used only by a passenger who is not operating the boat, while the operator attaches the engine shut-off cord to their PFD and scans constantly.
- The same notice says to confirm the shut-off valve is open before use, close it immediately if water leaks or another problem appears, and close the anchor light socket before use on 210FSH DELUXE.
- The To use the jet wash procedure says to connect the coil hose to the hose fitting, start the engines, push the jet wash switch, wait for water supply, adjust flow through 3 levels with the switch, and move the handle lever to discharge water.
- The stopping procedure says to push the jet wash switch, stop the engines, push the hose fitting collar inward, and disconnect the coil hose.
- The water supply procedure says to stop the engines, open the rear platform hatch, remove the inspection cover, turn the shut-off valve 90 clockwise to turn on or 90 counterclockwise to turn off, reinstall the cover, and close the hatch.
