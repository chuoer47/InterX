# Evidence Notes for 399

Question: What do you know about quick release button of a multi-use pressure cooker and air fryer?

Manual: `agentic-rag/en-manual/Instant Pot Duo Crisp.md`

Evidence:

1. `Instant Pot Duo Crisp.md:164-170` identifies the Quick Release Button, states that it controls the steam release valve and whether pressure is released, and says the Turn to Seal motion is a flick. It also warns not to turn the button more than 1/4 turn, or 45 degrees, and to let it spring back.
2. `Instant Pot Duo Crisp.md:236-248` explains Quick Release: it stops cooking quickly, helps prevent overcooking, is used for quick-cooking vegetables and delicate seafood, and is performed by pressing the quick release button down until it clicks and locks into Vent. It says steam ejects through the top of the steam release valve, the float valve drops when pressure is fully released, and hands, face, or exposed skin must not be placed over the steam release valve.
