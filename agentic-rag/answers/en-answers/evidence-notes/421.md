Question: What are the correct steps to turn using a snowmobile?

Manual: `agentic-rag/en-manual/Yamaha Snowmobile.md`

Evidence:

- `Yamaha Snowmobile.md:1051` says beginners should practice throttle, brake, and turning techniques in a large, flat area free of obstacles and traffic before more difficult terrain.
- `Yamaha Snowmobile.md:1071` is the `TURNING` section.
- `Yamaha Snowmobile.md:1073` states that for most snow surfaces, body positioning is key: slow down when approaching a curve, turn the handlebars in the desired direction, put weight on the inside running board, and lean the upper body into the turn.
- `Yamaha Snowmobile.md:1073` also says to practice at slow speed many times, lean more for sharper or faster turns, and avoid abrupt throttle changes, excessive braking, incorrect body movement, or too much speed because these may cause tipping.
- `Yamaha Snowmobile.md:1073` says that if the snowmobile begins to tip while turning, lean more into the turn, and if necessary gradually let off the throttle and/or steer to the outside of the turn.
