Question: How do I check the boat's steering system when I'm driving it?

Manual guess: `Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md`

Evidence:
- `Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md:1661-1667` says improper inspection or maintenance increases accident/damage risk, says not to operate if a problem is found, and frames these as pre-operation checks before operating.
- `Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md:1673-1687` says steering and shifting pre-operation checks require two people, one at the controls and one observing at the stern. It says to check the steering wheel for looseness/free play, turn it fully right and left for smooth unrestricted movement, verify both jet thrust nozzles change direction correctly, verify no wheel-to-nozzle free play, and verify the articulating keel follows the nozzles.
- `Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md:1963-1979` explains the driving behavior: steering depends on steering-wheel position and throttle; do not pull the remote control levers back to idle when steering away from objects; above trolling speed the boat rapidly loses steering response without throttle.
