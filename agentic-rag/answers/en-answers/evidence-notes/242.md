Question: How to find the approval label of emission control certificate of the boat?

Manual guess: Yamaha 210FSH 2021

Evidence:

- `agentic-rag/en-manual/Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md:135-149` states that the engine family conforms to EPA regulations and that the approval labels for the emission control certificate are attached to each engine unit and to the inside of the engine compartment. The adjacent figures `Manual09_6` and `Manual09_7` are captioned as emission control information label locations.
- `agentic-rag/en-manual/Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md:1057-1063` explains that the engine hood provides access to the engine, that the engine hood latches are under the front of the leaning post, and that the hood is opened by pulling the latches up and lifting the hood. It also warns that on 210FSH DELUXE the engine hood should not be opened while the anchor light is installed.

Answer rationale:

The question asks how to find the approval label, so the answer combines the label-location section with the engine-hood access section. The label figures are included because they show the marked label positions on the engine unit and inside the engine compartment.
