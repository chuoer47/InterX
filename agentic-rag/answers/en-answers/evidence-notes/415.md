Question: What is the proper way to use the brake lever and brake button?

Manual: Yamaha Snowmobile.md

Evidence:

1. Lines 440-446: The brake lever stops the machine by braking the entire drive system. Squeeze the brake lever toward the handlebar to stop the machine; the brake light illuminates when the brake is operated.
2. Lines 456-462: For parking or starting, apply the parking brake by squeezing the brake lever, pushing down the parking brake button while releasing the lever, and releasing it by squeezing the brake lever again. Warning: set the parking brake before starting, but never run with it applied because it can overheat the brake disc and reduce braking ability.
3. Lines 1065-1069: When slowing or stopping, release the throttle and apply the brake gently, not suddenly; improper brake use can cause the drive track to lose traction and reduce control.
