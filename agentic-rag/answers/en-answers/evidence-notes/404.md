# 404 Evidence Notes

Question: What is the correct procedure for cleaning the vacuum cleaner filter?

Manual: `agentic-rag/en-manual/Roomba i5.md`

Evidence:

1. `Roomba i5.md:178-190` states the care frequency table: the filter should be cared for once per week, twice per week in homes with pets, and replaced every 2 months.
2. `Roomba i5.md:206-218` gives the filter-cleaning procedure: remove the bin, pull out the filter by grasping both ends, tap debris from the filter against a rubbish bin, reinsert the filter with ridged grips facing out, and place the bin back in the robot. It also states the robot will not run if the filter is not installed correctly and to replace the filter every two months.
3. `Roomba i5.md:234-240` warns not to wash the filter and to remove the filter before washing the bin.
4. `Manual32_10.jpg` shows the filter location/removal from the bin.
5. `Manual32_11.jpg` shows tapping the filter over a rubbish bin.

Image decision:

Used `Manual32_10` and `Manual32_11` because they directly support the removal and tapping steps in the filter-cleaning procedure.
