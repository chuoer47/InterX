# Question 354

Question: How can you check the battery level of a landline?

Manual: `Philips XL490 XL495.md`

Evidence:

- `Philips XL490 XL495.md:311-317` has the "Check the battery level" section. It states that the battery icon displays the current battery level and notes that an almost-empty battery produces warning tones during a call before disconnection.
- `Philips XL490 XL495.md:315` anchors image `Manual22_28`, which shows the handset screen battery icon location.
- `Philips XL490 XL495.md:325-329` introduces display icons in standby mode and anchors image `Manual22_30`.
- Image `Manual22_30` explains that, off the base station/charger, the icon bars indicate battery level from full to low; on the base station/charger, the bars keep scrolling until charging is complete; an empty flashing battery icon with an alert tone means the battery is low and needs recharging.
