# Question 316 Evidence Notes

Question: "If I'm in Canada, is there any statement of the fax vailable in my country?"

Manual: `Brother Safety Guide.md`

Evidence:

1. Lines 339-345, `EQUIPMENT ATTACHMENT LIMITATIONS (Canada Only) (Models with the fax function only)`: states that the product meets the applicable Innovation, Science and Economic Development Canada technical specifications, and that REN indicates the maximum number of devices allowed on a telephone interface. Any combination is allowed only if the total REN does not exceed five.
2. Lines 347-349, `For Use in the USA or Canada Only`: states that these products are made for use in the USA and Canada only. Brother does not recommend overseas use because it may violate telecommunications regulations for fax models and power requirements may not be compatible; overseas use is at the user's risk and may void the warranty.

Images: none. The relevant evidence is regulatory text without a supporting figure.
