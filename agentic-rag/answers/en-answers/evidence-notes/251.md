# 251 Evidence Notes

Question: How do I turn on or off the water supply button on my boat?

Manual: `agentic-rag/en-manual/Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md`

Evidence:
- `Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md:1385-1393` identifies the Jet wash section and warns that the jet wash system must be used only by a passenger who is not operating the boat. The notice says to close the shut-off valve if the system will not be used for an extended period, verify it is open before use, and close it immediately if there is a leak or other problem.
- `Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md:1399-1409` says to connect the coil hose, start the engines, and push the jet wash switch. `Manual09_174` labels the jet wash switch.
- `Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md:1411-1413` says the water supply starts 5 seconds after the jet wash switch is pushed and the flow can be adjusted to 3 levels by pushing the switch.
- `Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md:1425-1429` says to stop using the jet wash by pushing the jet wash switch, stopping the engines, and disconnecting the coil hose. It then begins the water-supply on/off procedure: stop the engines, open the rear platform hatch, and remove the inspection cover.
- `Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md:1431-1437` says to turn the shut-off valve 90 degrees clockwise to turn the water supply on and 90 degrees counterclockwise to turn it off, then install the inspection cover and close the hatch. `Manual09_180` labels the shut-off valve behind the inspection cover.

Images:
- `Manual09_174`: labels the upper rocker switch as the jet wash switch.
- `Manual09_180`: labels the shut-off valve location behind the inspection cover.

Answer notes:
- The user says "water supply button"; the manual uses "jet wash switch" for button operation and "shut-off valve" for the water-supply on/off procedure, so the answer includes both.
- `<PIC>` count is 2 and images count is 2.
