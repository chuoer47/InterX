# Question 249

Question: How to install an anchor light while the boat is moving?

Manual guess: Yamaha 210FSH 2021

Evidence:

- `agentic-rag/en-manual/Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md:1121` warns not to open the lockable storage compartment while the boat is moving because the lid can block vision, close unexpectedly, or pinch someone.
- `agentic-rag/en-manual/Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md:1329` says the 210FSH DELUXE removable anchor light is stored in the lockable storage compartment and setup begins by opening that compartment.
- `agentic-rag/en-manual/Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md:1333` through `agentic-rag/en-manual/Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md:1349` give the removable-light setup sequence: remove the light, disconnect stoppers A and B, extend the pole, screw stopper A onto the center section, align the protrusion with the socket slot, install the light, and fit stopper B into the socket.
- `agentic-rag/en-manual/Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md:1321` gives the 210FSH SPORT fixed/T-top light use procedure: pull the lock tab, raise the light, and restore the lock tab to lock it.
- `agentic-rag/en-manual/Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md:317` says both bow and anchor lights must operate before dawn or after dusk, while the anchor light alone must be lit when anchored in the dark.
- `agentic-rag/en-manual/Yamaha 210FSH 2021.md:957` explains the navigation and anchor lights switch: upper side for `NAV` turns on bow plus anchor lights for night running; lower side for `ANC` operates the anchor light alone when anchored at night.

Answer decision:

The question asks for installation while the boat is moving, but the DELUXE installation procedure requires opening the lockable storage compartment, which the manual specifically forbids while underway. The answer therefore starts with the safety constraint, then gives the correct setup steps to perform before moving or after stopping, plus the running/anchored switch positions and SPORT exception.
