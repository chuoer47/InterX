Question: How can you lower the roll bar of a lawn mower?

Manual used: `agentic-rag/en-manual/Toro Z Master 4000.md`

Evidence:

1. Lines 527-545 describe ROPS safety: keep the roll bar fully raised and locked with the seat belt to avoid rollover injury or death; there is no rollover protection when the roll bar is down; lower it only when absolutely necessary; do not wear the seat belt when down; drive slowly and carefully; raise it as soon as clearance permits.
2. Lines 547-565 give the lowering procedure: remove the hairpin cotters and 2 pins, lower the roll bar to the down position, then install the 2 pins and secure them with the hairpin cotters.
