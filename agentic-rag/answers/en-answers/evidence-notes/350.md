# Evidence Notes - 350

Question: What is the max load of the jetski?

Manual: `Yamaha WaveRunner 2005.md`

Evidence:

- `agentic-rag/en-manual/Yamaha WaveRunner 2005.md:121` states that the watercraft is designed for the operator and one passenger, must not exceed 2 persons, and has a maximum load of 160 kg (353 lb). It defines load as the total weight of cargo, operator, and passenger.
- `agentic-rag/en-manual/Yamaha WaveRunner 2005.md:860` confirms not to operate the watercraft when the total weight exceeds 160 kg (353 lb), including any cargo.

Answer:

The maximum load is 160 kg (353 lb), counted as cargo plus operator plus passenger. Maximum persons aboard is 2: operator and one passenger.
