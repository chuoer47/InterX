# Question 391

Question: How can you set steam release valve for a multi-use pressure cooker and air fryer?

Manual: `agentic-rag/en-manual/Instant Pot Duo Crisp.md`

Evidence refs:
- `agentic-rag/en-manual/Instant Pot Duo Crisp.md:164-170` says the quick release button controls the steam release valve, shows the quick release button, and states that the lid says Turn to Seal but the motion is a flick; do not turn the button more than 1/4 turn, or 45 degrees, and let it spring back.
- `agentic-rag/en-manual/Instant Pot Duo Crisp.md:172-178` says the steam release valve sits loosely on the steam release pipe, steam ejects from the valve top when pressure is released, and the valve must not be covered or blocked.
- `agentic-rag/en-manual/Instant Pot Duo Crisp.md:224-248` says pressure must be released before opening, Quick Release is done by pressing the quick release button until it clicks and locks into Vent, steam ejects through the valve, the float valve drops when pressure is fully released, and hands/face/exposed skin must stay away from the valve.

- `Manual30_14`: labels the quick release button and shows Seal position as flick to seal and Vent position as press to vent.
