# Evidence Notes for 375

Question: How can you set the jumpers of a motherboard?

Manual: Motherboard.md

Evidence:
- `Motherboard.md:375-383` opens section 1.6 Jumpers and identifies the Clear RTC RAM 2-pin CLRTC header; `Manual25_33.jpg` shows the CLRTC header and pins.
- `Motherboard.md:387-395` gives the RTC RAM clearing procedure: turn off and unplug the computer, short the two pins with a metal object, plug in and turn on the computer, then hold `<Del>` during boot to enter BIOS Setup and reenter data.
- `Motherboard.md:397-399` adds exceptions: if the steps fail, remove the onboard battery and short the pins again; for overclocking hangs, use CPU Parameter Recall by shutting down and rebooting instead of clearing RTC.
- `Motherboard.md:401-405` explains the CPU Over Voltage jumper: pins 2-3 enable more CPU voltage, and pins 1-2 return to the default setting; `Manual25_34.jpg` labels the default/disable and enable positions.

- `Manual25_34`: CPU_OV jumper positions for default/disable and enable.
